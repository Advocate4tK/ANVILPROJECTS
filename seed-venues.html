Before running: Make sure your config.js includes VENUES: 'Venues' inside CONFIG.AIRTABLE_TABLES. Example:
AIRTABLE_TABLES: { ..., VENUES: 'Venues' }

50 CT Venues

Creates venue records (Venue ID, Venue Name, Address, City, State) for all known CT fields used in the scheduling system. Venues that already exist (matched by Venue ID) are skipped automatically.

This will add records to your live Airtable base. Safe to re-run — duplicates are skipped.

Patch Club Links

Links each venue to its club in the Clubs table. Run this once after seeding venues.

Seed Fields

Creates individual field records (Field #1, #2, etc.) within each venue. Currently includes: Addison Park (Glastonbury) — Fields 68–72. Safe to re-run — duplicates are skipped by Field ID.
