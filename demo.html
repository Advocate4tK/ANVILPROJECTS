Referee Portal

One Click Sends the Blast. Refs Do the Rest.

The assignor generates and sends a blast email to the entire referee pool in one action. Refs click the link, submit their availability, and the data flows straight into the workstation — sorted, filtered, and ready to assign.

Returning refs are pre-filled automatically. New refs build their profile on the first submission and never re-enter it again.

  • Assignor-triggered blast to all active refs
  • Returning refs pre-filled — no re-entry
  • Sorted by available time window and club preference
  • Refs select max games per day and preferred locations
  • Under-18 refs trigger automatic guardian notification
Assignment Engine

Assign the Right Ref to the Right Game

The workstation shows all games alongside available referees — already filtered by time window and club preference. Drag and drop officials onto games. No spreadsheets, no email chains, no guessing.

Multiple assignment templates handle different game formats — one-ref games, standard 3-official crews, tournament configurations. The ref panel shows experience level, certification, and availability in a single view.

  • Games organized by date, time, and age group
  • Ref pane pre-sorted by availability and club preference
  • Multiple templates — standard, tournament, single-ref
  • Inline ref profile lookup during assignment
  • Drag-and-drop interface with instant save
  • Full tournament mode for high-volume weekends
Club Portal

Every Club Gets Their Own Portal

Each club has a dedicated, branded portal for submitting home games directly to the assignor. No emails, no spreadsheets — games feed straight into the workstation the moment they're entered.

Clubs can upload up to 100 games at once for a full season drop. When something changes, change requests flow through the same portal — no outside communication needed.

  • Branded per club with their own secure link
  • Bulk upload — up to 100 games in a single submission
  • Date, time, venue, field, and age group per game
  • Games appear instantly in the assignor workstation
  • Change requests handled seamlessly through the same portal
  • Club sees their full game schedule and assignment status
Youth Sports Compliance

We Handle the Requirements So You Don't Have To

👨‍👩‍👦

Guardian Info Required

Any referee under 18 cannot submit availability without providing parent or guardian contact information. The form enforces this — no workaround, no bypass.

✉️

Parent Auto-Notified

Every time a minor submits an availability form, a copy is automatically emailed to the guardian on file. No manual follow-up. No paper trail to maintain.

🔒

Isolated by Club

Each assignor only sees their own clubs and referees. Data doesn't bleed across organizations. Access is controlled per account with role-based permissions.

Payment Tracking

Pay Refs Without the Headache

The payment portal shows every game, every referee, and every amount owed — organized by week and club. Venmo handles are stored on file so you can send payment and mark it done without leaving the tool.

  • Weekly view organized by game and position
  • Venmo handles and check preferences stored per referee
  • One-click Pay button opens Venmo directly
  • Mark Paid to close out each game instantly
  • Payment history tracked across the full season
