Assignment Engine

Assign the Right Ref to the Right Game

The workstation shows all games alongside available referees filtered by experience and certification. Drag and drop officials directly onto games — no spreadsheets, no emails.

  • Games organized by date and age group
  • Ref pane filtered by availability and cert level
  • Drag-and-drop assignment interface
  • Full tournament mode for high-volume weekends
Referee Portal

Refs Submit Their Own Availability

Referees get a simple, mobile-friendly form to submit their availability, experience level, and certification. No account needed — just a link sent by the assignor.

  • Available dates and time windows per day
  • Years of experience and certification level
  • Automatically matched against open games
  • Assignor sees new submissions instantly

Club Portal

Every Club Gets Their Own Portal

Each club has a dedicated portal for submitting home games directly to the assignor. No emails, no spreadsheets — games feed straight into the system the moment they're entered.

  • Branded per club or organization
  • Submit home games with date, time, and location
  • Games appear instantly in the assignor workstation
  • Change requests handled through the same portal
Payment Tracking

Pay Refs Without the Headache

The payment portal shows every game, every referee, and every amount owed — organized by week. Venmo handles are stored on file so you can pay and mark it done in seconds.

  • Weekly view organized by game and position
  • Venmo handles stored per referee
  • One-click Pay button opens Venmo directly
  • Mark Paid to close out each game instantly
