Additional Information

📋 How You Get Games

Submitting this form tells the assignor when you're available. Here's what happens next:

  1. Assignor reviews your availability and matches you to open games based on your schedule, certification level, and experience.
  2. You receive a game invitation through Central Assign — check your email and the app regularly.
  3. Accept or decline promptly. Games that sit unanswered get reassigned. If you accept and can no longer make it, contact the assignor immediately.
  4. First-season referees typically start as Assistant Referees (ARs) on the line. This is normal — it's how you build experience before running center.
  5. After every game, file your game report in Central Assign the same day. This is mandatory — it protects you and the club.

Questions about your assignments? Contact your assignor directly — don't wait.
